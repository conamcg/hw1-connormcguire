## The Problem

In my role, I review dozens of support tickets submitted by users related to system malfunctions, knowledge support needs, and access requests. The majority of these tickets can be resolved by contracted IT resources, and I only need to intervene when there is a potential system bug or an in-depth knowledge issue that requires my expertise. Manually reviewing every ticket to determine which ones need my attention is time-consuming and inefficient.

## How Generative AI Could Help

* **Ticket triage and summarization:** An AI assistant could rapidly scan all incoming tickets and generate a concise summary of key insights, flagging only those that require my direct attention rather than requiring me to read each one individually.
* **Priority and pattern detection:** AI could identify recurring issues or patterns across tickets, helping surface systemic bugs or knowledge gaps that might not be obvious when reviewing tickets one at a time.
* **Automated draft communications:** AI could draft follow-up email messages to users who submitted tickets as well as to the contracted IT resources responsible for resolution, saving time and ensuring consistent, professional communication.
